Tonga

  • New Parliament Complex Construction: Tonga is scheduled to start construction of a new expensive dollar Parliament complex on the Lake Lakalakaimonu Estate within the Tufumahina Royal Estate in Pea, Tongatapu. The project is expected to be completed by 2027. (Matangi Tonga)
  • Chess Grandmaster's Visit: A Chess Grandmaster named Nigel Short from the United Kingdom recently visited the Ocean of Light International School in Tonga. Nigel aims to encourage students to play chess for activities and to promote chess programs within Tongan schools. (Matangi Tonga)

Hawaii

  • Song Contest in the Kamehameha Schools: The class of seniors in the school of Kamehameha Kapalama brushed the 105th annual Song Contest. This event marked months of preparation and promoted the students' commitment to Hawaiian culture and music. (https://www.hawaiinewsnow.com)
  • Real Estates record-breaking sale: An estate in Kahala, oceanfront, has been sold for $65.75 million which sets a new record for residential property sales in Hawaii. Oceanfront's previous owners include the late billionaire Genshiro Kawamoto from Japan and Chris Hemmeter, a developer. (The Business Journal)
